⋆˚ʚɞ Description ⋆˚ʚɞ

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish that's hearty and flavorful. Here’s a basic recipe to make traditional lasagna:

⋆˚ʚɞ Ingredients ⋆˚ʚɞ

  • 1 lb (450g) ground beef
  • 1/2 lb (225g) ground pork (optional for more flavor)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2-3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 cup (240ml) beef broth or water
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup (60ml) olive oil

⋆˚ʚɞ For the Cheese Mixture: ⋆˚ʚɞ

  • 15 oz (425g) ricotta cheese
  • 1 1/2 cups (170g) shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup (50g) grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/4 cup (60ml) chopped fresh parsley (or 1 tbsp dried parsley)

⋆˚ʚɞ For Assembling: ⋆˚ʚɞ

  • Lasagna noodles (about 12 sheets, cooked according to package instructions)
  • Extra shredded mozzarella cheese (for topping)

⋆˚ʚɞ Steps ⋆˚ʚɞ

  1. Prepare the Meat Sauce
    • In a large skillet or saucepan, heat olive oil over medium heat.
    • Add chopped onions and cook until translucent.
    • Add garlic and cook for another 1-2 minutes.
    • Add ground beef and pork (if using) and cook until browned. Drain excess fat if needed.
    • Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, and beef broth.
    • Add dried basil, oregano, salt, and pepper.
    • Simmer the sauce on low heat for about 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  2. Prepare the Cheese Mixture:
    • In a bowl, combine ricotta cheese, 1 1/2 cups of shredded mozzarella, Parmesan cheese, egg, and parsley. Mix well.
  3. Cook the Lasagna Noodles
    • Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Drain and lay them out flat on a baking sheet or parchment paper to prevent sticking.
  4. Assemble the Lasagna:
    • Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
    • Spread a thin layer of meat sauce on the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish.
    • Place a layer of lasagna noodles over the sauce.
    • Spread a portion of the cheese mixture over the noodles.
    • Add another layer of meat sauce over the cheese mixture.
    • Repeat the layers (noodles, cheese mixture, sauce) until all ingredients are used, finishing with a layer of sauce.
    • Top with extra shredded mozzarella cheese.
  5. Bake the Lasagna:
    • Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes.
    • Remove the foil and bake for an additional 20-25 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbly and golden brown.
    • Let the lasagna cool for about 10-15 minutes before serving. This helps it set and makes it easier to cut.
